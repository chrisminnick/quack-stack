Interesting Facts About Ducks

Ducks have waterproof feathers that keep them warm and dry while swimming.

Ducklings are born with the ability to swim and follow their mother instinctively.

Many duck species migrate thousands of miles every year for breeding and warmer climates.

Ducks use various quacks and calls to communicate with each other.
